Create the JetEx Knowledge Hub Communication site and 7 document libraries with metadata. Existing team sites (Ops, Finance, Maintenance, etc.) are untouched — no hub association needed. Claude's M365 connector is the search layer, not SharePoint native search.

Create JetEx Knowledge Hub Communication site — jetexcellence.sharepoint.com/sites/JetExKnowledgeHub
Register site as a hub site in SharePoint admin center
Create 7 document libraries: Company, Operations, Maintenance, Finance, People & HR, Commercial, Regulatory

  • M365 connector configured and searching the hub
  • Staleness flow running

Phase 6 · Week 4 Onward
Rollout & Adoption
3 onboarding waves · AI Office Hours · feedback loop

Drive use without forcing it. Onboard in waves, keep Office Hours running, and triage every "Claude got it wrong" incident into a concrete improvement.

Wave 1 (Wk 4): Lauren + Data Engineer + 1–2 power users. Premium seats.
Wave 2 (Wk 6): Leadership + department heads. Standard seats, upgrade as needed.
Wave 3 (Wk 8): Rest of org, dept by dept. Each gets ≤30-min onboarding + first task.
Run AI Office Hours weekly — demos, troubleshooting, wins log
Create AI Office Hours Log in SharePoint — questions, answers, skill gaps
Triage every "Claude got it wrong" incident: missing content / stale content / skill gap / wrong trigger / user error
